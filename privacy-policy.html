Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At Dazl Style, we are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect and use your data. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the Dazl Style mobile application available on Android and iOS platforms.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Camera Permissions

Dazl Style requests camera permissions on both Android and iOS devices for the following specific purposes:

  • Clothing Item Photography: To enable you to photograph your clothing items for upload to your digital wardrobe
  • Profile Photo Upload: To allow you to take and upload profile pictures within the app

Important: We only access your camera when you explicitly choose to take a photo within the app. Camera access is never used for any other purpose, including background monitoring, facial recognition, or data collection beyond the specific photo you choose to capture.

1.2 Photo Storage

When you upload photos through the Dazl Style app:

  • Photos are stored securely on our servers to enable app functionality
  • Images are associated with your account to display your wardrobe and profile
  • We do not share your photos with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes
  • You maintain ownership of all photos you upload

1.3 Account Information

To provide you with personalized fashion recommendations and wardrobe management, we collect:

  • Email address and account credentials
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Style preferences and wardrobe data
  • App usage information to improve your experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to:

  • Provide Core Services: Enable wardrobe management, outfit creation, and style recommendations
  • Improve User Experience: Analyze app usage to enhance features and functionality
  • Communicate With You: Send important updates about your account and our services
  • Ensure Security: Protect your account and prevent unauthorized access
  • Comply With Legal Obligations: Meet regulatory requirements and respond to legal requests

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Platform-Specific Permissions

3.1 Android Permissions

On Android devices, Dazl Style requests the following permissions:

  • CAMERA: To capture photos of clothing items and profile pictures
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To save and access photos (on Android versions below 13)
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: To access photos from your gallery (Android 13+)
  • INTERNET: To sync your wardrobe and access cloud features

You can manage these permissions at any time through your device's Settings > Apps > Dazl Style > Permissions.

3.2 iOS Permissions

On iOS devices, Dazl Style requests:

  • Camera Access: To take photos of clothing and profile pictures
  • Photo Library Access: To select existing photos from your device

You can modify these permissions through Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera/Photos > Dazl Style on your iOS device.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Photos and personal data are stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to user data, and only when necessary for service provision
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but continuously work to maintain the highest standards.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your personal information and can exercise the following rights:

5.1 Access and Portability

You can access your personal data and wardrobe information at any time through the app. You may also request a copy of your data in a portable format.

5.2 Correction and Deletion

You can update or correct your profile information directly in the app. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at privacy@dazlstyle.com.

5.3 Permission Management

You can revoke camera and photo permissions at any time through your device settings. Note that this may limit certain app functionality.

5.4 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of promotional emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any marketing message or adjusting your notification preferences in the app.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Specifically:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account remains active
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements

7. Children's Privacy

Dazl Style is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@dazlstyle.com.

8. Third-Party Services

Dazl Style may integrate with third-party services to enhance functionality. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Analytics Services: We use analytics tools to understand app usage and improve performance
  • Cloud Storage: Photos and data are stored using secure cloud infrastructure providers
  • Payment Processors: If applicable, payment information is processed by third-party payment providers

We carefully select third-party partners who maintain strong privacy and security standards. However, we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. When we transfer your data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you through the app or via email
  • Provide you with an opportunity to review the changes

Your continued use of Dazl Style after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@dazlstyle.com

Website: Contact Form

Response Time: We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 48 hours

Questions About Privacy?

We're here to help. Contact us if you have any questions about how we protect your data.
